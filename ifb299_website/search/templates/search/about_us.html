About Us

We strive to make renting a car as simple and easy as driving your own car.

Renting a car brings you freedom and we want to help you find the right car for you at the right price. We have locations all over Australia and offer a wide range of vehicles that will suit each customers needs and preferences.

We offer a range of features to make sure you can find the right car for you:

  • Search: You can search the site for the car you want
  • Tailor your search: You can filter your results based on a criteria of your choosing
  • View detailed car info: You can view information in detail of all of our cars
  • Location info: You can view the locations of all our stores and the cars available at which locations using a Google Maps functionality
  • Recommendations: You can view what cars other customers have recommended and see a rating of their experiences with a car, this can help you choose the right car for you. You can also leave a rating of your experience with a car to help future customers
